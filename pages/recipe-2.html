A Recipe For Curing Homesick Blues

Are you feeling any of the following?

  • Missing home
  • In deep need of a hug
  • Hungry

Good News! The cure is listed below.

You'll need:

  1. Rotisserie Chicken
  2. Your favorite vinyl record
  3. High quality Tissues

NEXT

  1. You put that record on just loud enough to drown out the busy thoughts in your head, but quiet enough to keep inner peace.
  2. Place tissues within arms distance of your seat.
  3. Plate some rotisserie chicken on a plate
  4. Sit down at your spot, feed your body, have a nice deep cry. All while listening to the tunes that place you at home within yourself.
