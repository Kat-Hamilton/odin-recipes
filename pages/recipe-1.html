PB&J

Do you ever come home from a long day, starving, and want a meal that heals the soul? PB&J is the way to go!

With a low prep time, and minimal ingredients, this is a sure fire way to calm the mind and fill the stomach.

Ingredients

  • 2x slices of bread
  • 1Tbs peanut butter
  • 1Tbs jelly

Directions

  1. Toast both slices of bread to desired crispiness
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on the remaining slice of bread.
  4. Place both slices of bread on top of each other, lining up all four corners. Cut the sandwich on a diagonal resulting in two similar triangular shapes.
  5. Enjoy your nourishing sandwich.
